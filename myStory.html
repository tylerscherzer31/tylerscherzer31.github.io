My Story

Thank you for visiting my portfolio,

     My name is Tyler Scherzer, I am currently studying Computer Science at West Chester University located in West Chester, Pennsylvania! I started my studies at Montgomery County Community College, where I received an A.A.S in Information Technology. After completing my Associates Degree, I transfered to West Chester University where I have been studying Computer Science since 2019. I will be graduating from West Chester University in December of 2021, and I am seeking an entry level job in the field of Information Technology.

   While attending West Chester University, I was honored with the opportunity of becoming a member of Sigma Phi Epsilon fraternity. Throughout my time with Sigma Phi Epsilon, I learned the values of virtue, diligence, and brotherly love. These same core values are what led me to being voted the VP of Philanthropy in the Spring Semester of 2019. While serving as the VP of Philanthropy, I was able to reach out to my local community and raise money for charitable organizations and local small businesses. This opportunity taught me a lot about networking, organization, and customer relations which has helped me a lot as I transition from college to the corporate world.
