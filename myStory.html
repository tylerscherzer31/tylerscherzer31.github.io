My Story

Thank you for visiting my portfolio,

    My name is Tyler Scherzer, I recently graduated Cum Laude from West Chester University recieving my B.S in Computer Science. I started my studies at Montgomery County Community College, where I received an A.A.S in Information Technology prior to transfering to my alma mater. I recently accepted a position with Ferrilli where I am serving as an Associate Consultant. At Ferrilli we aim to enhance institutional efficency and enrich the student expierence. Ferrilli utilizes a holistic approach to align technology to institutional strategy and culture. At Ferrilli, relationships are the heart of what we do and we stake our reputation on it.

   While attending West Chester University, I was honored with the opportunity of becoming a member of Sigma Phi Epsilon fraternity. Throughout my time with Sigma Phi Epsilon, I learned the values of virtue, diligence, and brotherly love. These same core values are what led me to being voted the VP of Philanthropy in the Spring Semester of 2019. While serving as the VP of Philanthropy, I was able to reach out to my local community and raise money for charitable organizations and local small businesses. This opportunity taught me a lot about networking, organization, and customer relations which has helped me a lot as I transition from college to corporate.

   Thank you for taking the time out of your busy schedule to learn a little bit about myself! Please feel free to contact me via email or through any of the listed contact options at the bottom of the page!
